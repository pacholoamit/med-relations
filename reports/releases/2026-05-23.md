### Paid Search Missing Dates

Fixed five compounding issues causing paid search reports to stall at 2026-05-07: orphaned `PaidSearchDateUnit` rows with stale keys blocking upserts, campaigns with no end date triggering timeouts, a null-return crash on empty search streams, an off-by-one chunk slice, and `concat(false)` polluting the consolidated byDate array.

Fixed two production crashes in the paid-search-prisma6 job: campaigns linked to a different org's summary no longer dereferenced as undefined, and aggregated channel totals (Google + Bing) are now summed numerically instead of string-concatenated due to Prisma 6 returning Decimal objects.
