Fixed five compounding bugs that caused Paid Search reports to stop updating past 2026-05-07: stale unique-key conflicts blocking upserts, a 14-year date range triggered by Google's "no end date" sentinel value, null returns crashing the segmented data builder, a chunk-slice off-by-one dropping one record per thousand, and `concat(false)` injecting junk rows into consolidated metrics.
